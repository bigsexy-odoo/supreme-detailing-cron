⎯ SUPREME FINISHES

Restoration and protection.

The deeper work — for paint, glass, and lenses worth looking after.

Clay Bar Treatment

Pulls bonded contaminants from the paint — industrial fallout, tree sap, brake dust — leaving the surface glass-smooth and ready for protection. Recommended once a year, or before any paint protection or ceramic work.

Paint Protection

A synthetic sealant applied over clean, clayed paint. Adds gloss, makes future washes easier, and shields against UV, water spots, and light contaminants. A noticeable step up from a standard wax, without committing to full ceramic.

Ceramic Coating

The flagship finish. A hard ceramic layer bonded to the paint for years of deep gloss, hydrophobic water-shedding, and resistance to swirls, oxidation, and contaminants. Quoted on the car — size, condition, and prep level all factor in.

Headlight Restoration

UV damage turns headlights cloudy and yellow, dimming night-driving visibility and ageing the look of the car. We wet-sand, polish, and seal the lenses so they're clear again — often saving the cost of a full headlight replacement.

⎯ INTERIOR SPECIALTIES

Beyond the vacuum.

The specialty interior work for pets, persistent smells, and properly-soiled carpets.

Pet Hair Removal

Pet hair gets woven into upholstery and carpet fibres and ignores normal vacuuming. We use specialist tools and techniques to lift it out properly — including the spots most washes don't reach.

Deep Carpet Clean

Hot-water extraction — shampoo and pull embedded dirt, old spills, and grime out of carpets, mats, and upholstery. Goes well beyond what vacuuming alone can do. Allow 4–6 hours drying time.
